Energy Intensive Industry Product-Level GHG Measurement

The standards stack for carbon & methane intensity

There is no single product-level GHG standard for the energy and emissions intensive industries — there is a stack. Horizontal LCA grammar at the base, an industry-specific estimation substrate above it, the fast-moving product-attribute layer where the commercial action sits, and the regulatory forcing functions turning voluntary methods into market-access requirements.
