Energy Intensive Industry Product-Level GHG Measurement

The standards stack for carbon intensity of energies and products

There is no single product-level GHG standard for the energy and emissions intensive industries — there is a stack. Horizontal LCA grammar at the base, an industry-specific estimation substrate above it, the fast-moving product-attribute layer where the commercial action sits, and, at the top, the forcing functions — not regulation alone, but disclosure, capital-market commitments and buyer demand — that turn voluntary measurement into market-access requirements, and increasingly into priced, tradable markets.
